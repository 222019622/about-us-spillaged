About Us

Who Are We?

We are an innovative platform committed to creating a cleaner, healthier, and sustainable environment for our communities.

What We Do

We act as a bridge between citizens and local authorities by providing a platform that integrates and supports continuous efforts to address water-supply crises and unattended sewage bursts that cause environmental defects in our communities.

How We Do It

Our mobile application is designed to empower citizens to play an active role in managing essential resources. By quickly and easily reporting water supply pipeline leakages, aging water-supply infrastructure, and sewage bursts, users can help local authorities address these issues promptly.

Our Goals

Societal

  • Building a stronger and more resilient community through technology and citizen engagement.
  • Reducing the risk of waterborne diseases associated with contaminated water.

Environmental

  • Timely reporting of sewage bursts prevents contamination of water bodies and soil, protecting ecosystems.

Economic

  • Minimizing water wastage and reducing repair costs.
  • Providing valuable data for urban planning and infrastructure development.

Our Vision

A developed South Africa where water as a precious resource is managed efficiently and effectively through citizen engagement and technology.
