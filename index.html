About Us

Discover Our Team's Story

It was a typical afternoon in Johannesburg when a University of Johannesburg student, let's call him Thabo, was on his way home. As he walked down the bustling streets, something caught his eye—a burst pipe spilling precious water onto the pavement. The water gushed out, creating a small river that wound its way into the street, but to Thabo’s surprise, no one seemed to notice. People walked by as if it were just another ordinary day.

But Thabo knew better. He understood the importance of every drop. He thought of the communities where water shortages were a daily struggle, where every drop was life. As he stood there, he wondered why no one was doing anything. Why wasn’t there a simple way to report this and get it fixed quickly?

More About Our Journey

SpillAged isn’t just an app—it’s a movement. A movement that empowers people like Thabo, and like you, to take action and make a difference in your community. Our platform bridges the gap between citizens and local authorities, making it easy for you to report issues like water-supply crises and unattended sewage bursts with just a few taps on your phone. But we didn’t stop there. We believe in rewarding those who step up. Every time you report an issue through SpillAged, you’re not just helping your community; you’re earning electronic tokens that can be redeemed for vouchers or promotions. It’s our way of saying thank you for caring, thank you for being part of the solution. Our mission is simple yet powerful: to create a cleaner, healthier, and more sustainable environment for everyone.


Whether it’s preventing water wastage, protecting our ecosystems, or reducing the risks associated with contaminated water, we’re in this together. Join us in building a stronger, more resilient community where every voice matters, and every action counts. Because with SpillAged, you’re not just reporting an issue—you’re taking the first step toward a better future. Get Involved. Make a Difference. SpillAged.

Our Services

Leak & Burst Reporting

Users can instantly report water pipe leaks or sewage bursts in their area through the app, providing detailed descriptions and location information.

Real-Time Incident Tracking

Track the status of reported issues in real-time, from initial submission to final resolution, ensuring transparency and keeping users informed about repair progress.

Direct Communication with Municipal Services

The app provides a direct communication channel between citizens and the Johannesburg municipality, allowing quicker and more efficient handling of water infrastructure issues.

Water Conservation Tips and Alerts

Educational content and push notifications to help users reduce water waste, alongside alerts during water shortages or municipal updates.

Incident History

Access a log of previously reported incidents in the user's area, helping citizens stay informed about recurring problems and overall maintenance.

Emergency Response Coordination

In case of severe leaks or bursts, the app offers contact information and procedures for emergency water shut-offs or quick municipal intervention.
